WWCKL - Fugee School Project

Fugee School Mentors Alil' community project ...

We started our journey from Aug 2016 - Dec 2016 to teach HTML/CSS Web Development to the refugee students in Fugee. Our mentors were rostered in pairs and take turns to prepare materials and organise the teaching amongst themselves.

There were no rewards, just sheer joy of teaching. Hence, we (WWCKL) decided to dedicate this page to all our mentors, helpers and volunteers. It's a platform for us to express our heartfelt thank you for all of you who have volunteered your time & effort for the last 3 months. We are forever grateful!
